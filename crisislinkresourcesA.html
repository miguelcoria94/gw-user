What can I do now?

If you're thinking about suicide or would like emotional support, you can talk to the following emergency services at anytime.

Dial 911 to communicate about where you are and send someone to support you immediately. On the Phone ask for someone who is on the Crisis Intervention Team (CIT).

Call 911

Dial 1-800-273-8255 to reach the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline. Trained professionals are on call 24/7 to speak with individuals who are having thoughts of self harm, suicide, or dying.

1-800-273-8255

Text "hello" to 741-741 to reach the Crisis Text Line. Trained professionals are available 24/7 to text message with individuals who are having thoughts of self harm, suicide, or dying.

741-741
